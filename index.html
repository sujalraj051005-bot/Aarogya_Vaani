How AarogyaVaani Works
The complete patient journey — from pre-arrival to weekly care

One platform. Four modules. Every stage of patient care.

AarogyaVaani calls patients in their own language — Hindi, Bhojpuri, Marathi, Bengali — before they arrive, after they leave, and every week in between. Every conversation becomes structured clinical data.

🏥
Pre-Arrival Triage
AI calls patient 2–4 hrs before OPD. Collects complaint, pain scale, urgency. Pre-fills doctor's intake form.
🏠
Post-Discharge
24 & 48 hr follow-up calls. Checks prescription, discharge understanding, danger signs.
💊
Chronic Coach
Weekly calls for diabetes & hypertension. Meds, diet, symptoms. Builds longitudinal PRO dataset.
👨‍👩‍👧
Caregiver Proxy
For elderly or low-literacy patients, routes the call to the family caregiver instead.
